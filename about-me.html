🏗️ I am a Civil and Hydraulic Engineering Technician, now retired.
✨ What brings me the most joy in retirement is sharing mathematical concepts, writing practical engineering programs, and creating mathematical 3D graphics.

Throughout years of engineering practice, I realized that many engineering computation routines or 2D/3D mathematical plots can be effectively implemented using Scratch or TurboWarp. These platforms are not only practical and visual, but also greatly lower the learning curve, making abstract mathematical ideas tangible and engaging.

This repository MCA-WITHOUT-LOOKUPTABLE embodies that vision:

  • ✅ A pure geometric interpretation of Marching Cubes — no traditional 256-case lookup table
  • ✅ Natural dimensional extension from 2D Marching Squares to 3D implicit surfaces
  • ✅ Efficient Python implementation, plus ready adaptation for Scratch/TurboWarp for educational demonstrations

I firmly believe: Visualization is understanding. Whether it’s structural analysis, fluid mechanics, or mathematical surfaces, turning abstract symbols into graphics allows more people to enjoy the joy of exploration.

🧪 Tech stack: Python + NumPy + Matplotlib  |  🎮 TurboWarp / Scratch 3.0
